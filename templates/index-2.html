Avīci (01)

Avīci (01) is the fourth and final extended play by Swedish DJ and record producer Avicii, serving as the last release in his lifetime. It was produced by Avicii, Benny Blanco, Andrew Watt, Cashmere Cat and Carl Falk.

Released : 10 August 2017
Genre : progressive house; dance-pop; folktronica
Length : 17:43
Producer : Avicii; Benny Blanco; Andrew Watt; Cashmere Cat; Carl Falk

New Blood

New Blood is the third studio album from Dutch electronic music duo Yellow Claw.Dancing Astronaut quoted that the album felt "more diverse in scope than what one would usually experience at a Yellow Claw set" and consisted of tracks which "fall in line with their signature sound without stretching too far outside their comfort zone".

Released :22 June 2018
Genre : Future bass; trap
Length : 44:46
Producer : Yellow Claw; San Holo; DJ Snake; Chace Moksi

Kygo

The album's first single, "Firestone", featuring Australian singer Conrad Sewell, was released on 1 December 2014.
